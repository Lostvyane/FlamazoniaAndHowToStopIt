How To Help Fight The

AMAZON FIRES

Restoring the Amazon rainforest is not going to be easy but the good news is that many are willing to contribute. It will take the efforts of many people working together in order to ensure that rainforests and their wildlife will survive for your children to appreciate, enjoy, and benefit from.

There has already recently been a climate stike all around the world addressing the effects of climate change which will hopefully help open the eyes of the public to everything we are doing wrong as people and to the possible future of our home if we don't act.
